More Than Half of adults drink ALCOHOL to cope with STRESS!

Almost 60 per cent of adults drink alcohol in order to deal with everyday life stress, a study has discovered.

Researches have taken place conducted by Drink aware investigated the normal drinking pattern of people in the UK aged between 18 and 75. Around 6,000 adults have been assessed through a course of a few weeks between March and April last year.

The results of the study have revealed majority of reason why people chose to drink alcohol. 40 per cent of male and females participants have said that the reason why they sometimes drink alcohol was to forget their problems, the other 41 per cent also noted down that they drink occasionally when they are feeling depressed or nervous.
