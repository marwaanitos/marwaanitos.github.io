Further Support

1.Mind UK
Mind UK is one of the leading sites that help deal with depression and stress on a daily basis, they have a number of stories from real people dealing with these problem on a daily basis, they aslo have a section specifically for family and friends of suffers who also seek help.

2. NHS

The National Health Service is the publicly funded national healthcare system for England and one of the four National Health Services of the United Kingdom. It is the largest single-payer healthcare system in the world and therefore will be one of the most benificial for country and world-wide help for depression.

3. signs of depression - Mirror

The mirror is one of todays biggest newspaper, the information is sorced from a large range of people around the world before publishing an article. this article helps someone who is worried for a friend or family member showing signs of depression due to stress.

4. Workplace and depression

This site shows how depression in the work place can occur and what you can do as a staff member or boss can do to keep your colligues/ employees happy while working.

5.Talk about depression

this is an online site being used to get medical advice, support and help to fight against depression on the work place. There is a number of people ready to talk right now!
