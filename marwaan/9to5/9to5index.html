Welcome to 9 to 5 Worker Stress!


This is the 9 to 5 Worker Stress website!

Aiming to help out those stressed and point them in the right direction!

Stress can be harmful to your health and may cause serious illness. Excessive stress can interfere with your productivity and performance at work by impacting your physical and emotional state and affect your relationship at home.

Stress can lead to success and failure on individual's job. Many workers feel unable to control the work environment and feels helpless while stuck under difficult situation.

There are steps worker can take to protect themselves from damaging effect of stress by identifying the problem and preventing them getting worse by taking a simple stress test on this website.

The test will help individual worker highlight the problem areas and will provide the link to the relevant organisations where they can receive help from!
