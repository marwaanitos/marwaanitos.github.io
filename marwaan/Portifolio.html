Talk to Amsterdam

Talk to Amsterdam" is virtual reality experience that allows the player to explore amsterdam and talk with the NPC's as if they are people. This is an attempt to make learning new languages easier and more natural than traditional ways.

Sites like "duolingo" offer a variety of ways to learn a new language using complex algorithms to keep learners engaged and help them complete their journey in learning a new language.

My project offers a alternative approach by turning learning languages more into a real experience rather than a densely compressed course.
